About TPAA

A group of Texas pipeline operators with one shared goal: keeping Texans safe around pipelines.

Established in 2006, the Texas Pipeline Awareness Alliance (TPAA) is a collaborative group of Texas pipeline operators focused on increasing public awareness about pipelines and pipeline safety. TPAA uses radio, TV, magazine, newspaper and web campaigns to communicate safety messages to Texas residents in all 254 counties — in English and Spanish.

What we want every Texan to know

  • Contact 811 at least two working days before any digging project — it's free, and it's the law
  • Know the sight, sound and smell signs of a pipeline leak
  • If you suspect a leak: leave the area, then call 911 and the pipeline operator from a safe distance

911

Suspect a pipeline leak?

Leave the area on foot immediately and warn others. From a safe distance, call 911 and the pipeline company on the marker. Don’t use phones, switches, vehicles or anything that could spark near the leak.
