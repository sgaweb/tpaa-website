Call 811 before you dig.

More than 480,000 miles of pipeline run under Texas — more than any other state. One free call at least two working days before you dig, plant or build keeps you, your family and your community safe. It’s also the law.

About the alliance

Who we are

Established in 2006, the Texas Pipeline Awareness Alliance (TPAA) is a group of Texas pipeline operators focused on increasing public awareness about pipelines and pipeline safety. TPAA uses radio, TV, print and the web to bring safety messages to residents in all 254 Texas counties.

911

Suspect a pipeline leak?

Leave the area on foot immediately and warn others. From a safe distance, call 911 and the pipeline company on the marker. Don’t use phones, switches, vehicles or anything that could spark near the leak.
